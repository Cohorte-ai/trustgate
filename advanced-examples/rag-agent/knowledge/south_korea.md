# South Korea

- Capital: Seoul
- Population: 51.7 million (2024)
- Area: 100,210 km²
- GDP: $1.71 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Won (KRW)
- Official language: Korean
- Continent: Asia
- Life expectancy: 83.7 years
- Largest city: Seoul (9.7 million city proper, 25.5 million metro)
- Government: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
