# Mexico

- Capital: Mexico City
- Population: 129.4 million (2024)
- Area: 1,964,375 km²
- GDP: $1.79 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Peso (MXN)
- Official language: Spanish
- Continent: North America
- Life expectancy: 75.1 years
- Largest city: Mexico City (9.2 million city proper, 21.8 million metro)
- Government: Federal presidential constitutional republic
