# Egypt

- Capital: Cairo
- Population: 112.7 million (2024)
- Area: 1,001,450 km²
- GDP: $0.40 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Pound (EGP)
- Official language: Arabic
- Continent: Africa
- Life expectancy: 72.1 years
- Largest city: Cairo (10.1 million city proper, 21.3 million metro)
- Government: Unitary semi-presidential republic
