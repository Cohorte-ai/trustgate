# Japan

- Capital: Tokyo
- Population: 123.3 million (2024)
- Area: 377,975 km²
- GDP: $4.23 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Yen (JPY)
- Official language: Japanese
- Continent: Asia
- Life expectancy: 84.8 years
- Largest city: Tokyo (13.9 million city proper, 37.4 million metro)
- Government: Unitary parliamentary constitutional monarchy
