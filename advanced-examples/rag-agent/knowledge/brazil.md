# Brazil

- Capital: Brasilia
- Population: 216.4 million (2024)
- Area: 8,515,767 km²
- GDP: $2.13 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Real (BRL)
- Official language: Portuguese
- Continent: South America
- Life expectancy: 76.1 years
- Largest city: São Paulo (12.3 million city proper, 22.0 million metro)
- Government: Federal presidential constitutional republic
