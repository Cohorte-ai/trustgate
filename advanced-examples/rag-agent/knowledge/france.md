# France

- Capital: Paris
- Population: 67.75 million (2024)
- Area: 551,695 km²
- GDP: $2.78 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Euro (EUR)
- Official language: French
- Continent: Europe
- Life expectancy: 82.5 years
- Largest city: Paris (2.1 million city proper, 12.3 million metro)
- Government: Unitary semi-presidential republic
