# Russia

- Capital: Moscow
- Population: 143.8 million (2024)
- Area: 17,098,242 km²
- GDP: $1.86 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Ruble (RUB)
- Official language: Russian
- Continent: Europe/Asia
- Life expectancy: 73.2 years
- Largest city: Moscow (12.6 million city proper, 17.1 million metro)
- Government: Federal semi-presidential constitutional republic
