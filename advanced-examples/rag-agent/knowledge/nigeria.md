# Nigeria

- Capital: Abuja
- Population: 223.8 million (2024)
- Area: 923,768 km²
- GDP: $0.47 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Naira (NGN)
- Official language: English
- Continent: Africa
- Life expectancy: 53.9 years
- Largest city: Lagos (15.4 million city proper, 21.3 million metro)
- Government: Federal presidential constitutional republic
