# India

- Capital: New Delhi
- Population: 1,441.7 million (2024)
- Area: 3,287,263 km²
- GDP: $3.94 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Rupee (INR)
- Official languages: Hindi, English
- Continent: Asia
- Life expectancy: 70.8 years
- Largest city: Mumbai (12.5 million city proper, 21.0 million metro)
- Government: Federal parliamentary constitutional republic
