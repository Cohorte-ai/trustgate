# Canada

- Capital: Ottawa
- Population: 40.1 million (2024)
- Area: 9,984,670 km²
- GDP: $2.14 trillion (2024)
- Currency: Dollar (CAD)
- Official languages: English, French
- Continent: North America
- Life expectancy: 82.7 years
- Largest city: Toronto (2.8 million city proper, 6.2 million metro)
- Government: Federal parliamentary constitutional monarchy
